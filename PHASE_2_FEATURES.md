# Phase 2 Features

These upgrades are intentionally deferred until after launch so the production storefront stays stable and lightweight.

- Professional domain email setup, such as `info@titanopticals.com`, after domain purchase. Keep the client-provided Gmail live until then.
- Online payment integration for Nepal payment methods.
- SMS, email, or WhatsApp automation for order and booking updates.
- Real customer reviews and testimonials after collecting approved customer content.
- Wishlist/save-for-later with saved products in customer accounts.
- Multiple product images and gallery database support.
- Virtual try-on or 3D frame preview if performance and assets justify it.
- Coupons and discount codes.
- Printable invoice generation.
- Advanced inventory reporting and low-stock alerts.
- SEO blog or eyewear advice section.
- Analytics dashboard for sales, products, and customer inquiries.
- Server-side pagination and search if the product catalog grows beyond a small launch catalog.
- Advanced animation ideas such as tasteful hero parallax or Lottie only if real brand assets are available.
